Strategic Signals

Real-world flags raised by the model.
South Carolina tax methodology — SC charges no sales tax on vehicles. Instead, the Infrastructure Maintenance Fee (IMF) is 5% of sale price capped at $500, paid once at registration on both purchases AND leases (S.C. Code § 56-3-627). Vehicles are subject to annual personal property tax: Fair Market Value × 6% assessment ratio × millage rate ÷ 1000. Greenville County millage typically falls between 300–360 mills depending on tax district. Property tax declines yearly as the vehicle depreciates per NADA values. Title fee = $15, plate/registration ≈ $40 (passenger), $60 (hybrid), $120 (EV — SC charges higher EV registration to recoup gas tax). Doc fees vary by dealer (typically $300–$700) and must be published annually by January 31. Lease acquisition fees ≈ $695, disposition fees ≈ $395. Always verify current rates with SCDMV and the Greenville County Auditor before signing.
